Profile

Ilham Karim (born 1999, Jakarta; raised in Palembang) is an artist based in Yogyakarta. He focuses on painting, exploring the canvas through diverse forms and compositions. His work is influenced by digital culture and his multicultural family identity, presenting artistic exploration as a personal language to interpret the past, present and possible futures. The bold use of colours, figures and materials in his work reflects contemporary aesthetics and ideas. For Karim, painting represents more than just a task in front of a canvas; it is a deliberate effort to create a connection between the work, the audience, the space, and the context.

Since beginning his artistic journey in the Fine Arts Department at the Indonesian Institute of the Arts Yogyakarta in 2019, Karim has exhibited extensively in Indonesia and internationally, including in Japan, Korea, Taiwan, and the United Kingdom. He also co-founded the Tikum Forum collective in 2020. In 2023, he was selected as a finalist for UOB Painting of The Year, and in 2024, he held his debut solo exhibition, Butterflies In My Stomach, at Sewu Satu Gallery in Jakarta.
